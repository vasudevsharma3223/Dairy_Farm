Our Passion for Pure Dairy

Founded by Vasudev — Nurturing Purity Since 2011

At Meadowbrook Organic Dairy, we believe in restoring the ancient purity of milk. Founded by Vasudev and located in the lush pastures of Kolkata, our farm is home to purebred Gir and Sahiwal cows that graze freely on organic fodder.

Our commitment to quality ensures that zero hormones, chemical preservatives, or water adulterants ever touch our milk. We combine traditional bilona churning and modern hygienic processing to deliver health directly to your doorstep.

  • 100% Organic Pasture Fodder
  • Zero Hormones or Antibiotics
  • Bilona Churned Ghee
  • Cold Chain Lock Delivery
